Mokita — Web
UI Design, UX Design, Communication Design


Breaking information asymmetry in the Forex market

Mokita or "truth that everybody knows but agrees to not talk about" — provides a comprehensive financial tool for expats who have commitment or retirement ambitions back home. Mokita exploits information asymmetry as an opportunity to level out the playing field between providers and end-users.


The challenge

Instill trust in Mokita users — which is always challenging in a financial context. Communicate cleary should the user transfer their funds now — and which transfer-service they should use.

My process

Initially, detailed interviews with stakeholders were carried out to understand their competition, vision, and goals for the product and the brand — near and long term. The MVP was designed and tested via inVision — with a round of revisions based on user testing.


Solutions

PREDICT based on macro FX trends SEARCH the best FX provider PERSONALIZE FX needs—alerts on when you should transfer and how much. SINGLE CLICK SME FX payments

Brand design

A detailed questionnaire was used to recognize how the clients saw the Mokita brand. This included questions such as "If Mokita was a car brand, which one it would be?", and "Is Mokita accessible or exclusive?".

Blue color was used to instill trust, and an arrow was used as a building element — communicating direct and fast transfers.

I designed detailed brand guidelines as well.
