Alias Words — iOS, Android
UI Design, UX Design


The perfect social game for your next party — available on iOS and Android.

Players have to describe a given word using its synonyms, without using the root word. Alias is available in 5 languages and has been installed over 300.000 times. Currently, there are over 80.000 active players.



The challenge

Translating the fun spirit of the game to a mobile app. Most users already knew the rules but had 2 main concerns — whose turn it is to explain the words, and how much game time remains.

My process

Research into similar apps guided the direction of the design. Detailed analysis and playing the game helped me identify the main pain point — the time from opening the app to playing a game.


Solutions

I branded the app with vivid colors, patterns, and playfull animations representing the fun spirit of the game. The most common use case — a game with 2 teams — can be started in two taps. A timer clearly shows the remaining time, and instructions before every round inform the users about player order.


Inviting design

Homescreen is playful with background animation, inviting users to start a new game. Secondary options are presented on the bottom.

Users can add their own words

Play a game

Setting up a new game can be done in as little as two taps. Two teams are prefilled by default since that is the most common team count.

Teams are deleted through engaging interaction — acompanied with fun animations.

Real fun

A timer is displayed numerically and graphically — a horizontal bar decreases in height as time goes by. Clear indicator on active player is presented on the bottom.

Players can edit their answers after the round ends, if there were any mistakes.
