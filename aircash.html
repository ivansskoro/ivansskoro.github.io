iOS & Android, UI & UX, 2020

Aircash is a new kind of financial institution on the market — a digital wallet offering easy top-up and money withdrawal — all without a bank account.

Owner of EU electronic money license, and with over 50.000 users and €13mil/year in transactions — coupled with tight integration with many Croatian service providers — Aircash is one of the main players on the Croatian fintech scene.

Homescreen, settings and parking payment

Brief

The existing version of the app was in need of a complete rework. Architecture was based on Cordova and design was an afterthought, taken care of by the development team.

Information architecture was non-existent — there was no clear hierarchy guiding the user through the experience.


Process overview

Working through analytics data with stakeholders, I identified the most used features of the app and decided on a roadmap  for redesign. The lack of visual consistency and information architecture was tackled first.

Following that, I worked on defining, designing, and expanding the services section — our main source of revenue and customer growth.

Special attention was given to the new user experience by designing new onboarding and login sections.
