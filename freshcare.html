Fresh Care — iOS, Android
UI Design, UX Design


Empowering senior family members with intelligent electricity meters.

Fresh Care analyzes seniors' electricity usage via an intelligent electricity meter. An algorithm detects their wellbeing and sends a daily report to their family members.



The challenge

Take existing hardware and consumption data and ideate a new product based on a general idea — making seniors independant and safe.

My process

First, I facilitated workshops with the data science team to identify data and hardware limitations. Following that, user journeys based on our personas were used to define this innovative product within tight constraints.


Solutions

During ideation and research phase, we concluded that the most critical time in the day of a senior is the morning — if something is wrong, electricity consumption won't be average. Algorithm detects if there are anomalies in consumption during the morning — the end user  (family member) can set the exact time in which the algorithm will run.

Compared to competitors, Fresh Care requires no cameras or additional hardware — making seniors feel independant and empowered. If there is a problem, family members immediately get notified via SMS.


Personas and user journeys

To define the product, we used personas and user journey maps — good and bad scenarios. This helped us pinpoint morning as the most important time.
