Roometric — iOS, Android, Public website
UI Design, UX Design, Communication Design


Designing in Augmented Reality to decrease illegally high rents and plan interiors.

Based in Berlin, Roometric provides mobile measurement solutions. Their apps generate 3D interior scans with only a smartphone. Scans an be imported into interior planing solutions or used to calculate if the rent is illegally high.



The challenge

Redesign a complicated AR scanning process with too many steps. There was no clear flow and major inconsistencies in the information architecture presented problems for the users — identical elements behaved differently on different screens, and back/cancel hierarchy was not consistent.

My process

Observing users while they used the app and getting informed about the technical limitations of the software helped me define a new user flow. Each revision of the main feature — the scaning process — was tested with 3 users. The rest of the app was structured and designed following regular UI & UX practices. I complimented the new design with a public website.


Solutions

A step-by-step wizard was implemented to simplify the scaning process. Information structure was revised with success messages and improved with guides and scaning tips. Users were provided with consistent back functionality and warnings for destructive actions.

Number of steps in the main flow was decreased, speeding up the scaning process.


Flow diagram

Based on user observations a new flow diagram was designed  for future reference. Step-by-step scan instructions guide the user through the most complicated flow in the app.

A detailed flow diagram helped me to identify the main issues in the flow and streamline the scanning process. Users were provided with consistent back functionality and warnings for destructive actions — all missing in the previous version.

AR scanning

Users can see all of their measured appartments and easily create a new measurement.

Most of our users are not familiar with AR, so it is important to guide them and provide them with constructive feedback.

All measurements taken by users are displayed in real time AR, providing users with feedback.

After the scan

User gets a full 3D model with floor and wall area surface calculated for him.

Potential rent savings are communicated to the user. The app compares the rent to the law defined limit for that area and housing size.

Scan with all measurements and elements (doors, windows etc.) can be uploaded to 3rd party services. Users can export a file or scans a QR code inside 3rd party desktop software for interior planing. The app injects the URL with correct parameters making the scan available on desktop. After that, he can easily share the link with his interior designer.

Design system

A minimal amount of colors and elements puts the focus on the AR experience.

Public website

The new visual language was applied to a simple landing page — communicating the feature set and advantages of Roometric
